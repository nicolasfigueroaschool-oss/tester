AIR MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

The Stage

Air Man’s stage takes place high in the sky among the clouds. You’ll spend most of your time hopping across floating platforms and riding on the backs of giant "Gobots" (large fan-headed robots). The real danger here is the wind and the many bottomless pits; one mistimed jump or a gust of wind from an enemy can send you falling to your doom. High-speed bird robots also fly across the screen to knock you off balance.

The Boss

Air Man is a bulky robot with a massive fan embedded in his torso. He doesn't move around much, but he is a master of "walling" you in. He generates several small tornadoes that drift across the screen in various patterns, making it extremely difficult to approach him without taking damage. After firing three waves of tornadoes, he will leap to the other side of the room to reset the cycle.

Weakness and Strategy

Air Man is extremely weak to the Leaf Shield (obtained from Wood Man). Activating the shield not only protects you from his smaller tornadoes but also deals massive damage when thrown at him. If you don't have the Leaf Shield, use your standard Mega Buster and stay as close to him as possible to slip through the gaps in his tornado patterns.

Quick Tip

Don't be intimidated by the wall of tornadoes! There is almost always a small "blind spot" or gap in the pattern. If you find yourself stuck, it is often better to take one hit from a tornado and use the brief period of invincibility to run right through the rest of them and spam your attack directly into his face.
