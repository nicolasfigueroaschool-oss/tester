WOOD MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

The Stage

Wood Man’s stage is a dense, robotic forest teeming with mechanical wildlife. You’ll have to fight off fire-breathing robotic dogs, giant ostriches, and monkeys that drop from the trees. The path is linear but requires high damage output to clear the heavy enemies blocking your way.

The Boss

Wood Man is a sturdy robot made of cypress wood. He protects himself with the Leaf Shield, a rotating circle of leaves that blocks almost all incoming projectiles. He attacks by tossing the shield at you while simultaneously making sharp leaves fall from the ceiling.

Weakness and Strategy

His absolute greatest weakness is Atomic Fire (from Heat Man). A fully charged shot can take him down in just two hits. If you don't have that, the Metal Blade or Crash Bomber can pierce or damage his shield effectively.

Pro Tip

Timing is everything. Wait for Wood Man to jump; that is usually the moment he lowers his guard to throw his shield. If you are using the Mega Buster, stay at a medium distance so you have enough time to hop over the shield as it slides across the floor.
