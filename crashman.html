CRASH MAN

The Stage

Crash Man’s stage is a vertical climb through a series of high-altitude platforms and long ladder sections. You’ll spend most of your time on moving rails and dodging "Telly" robots that swarm out of dispensers. The real challenge is the "Propeller Eye" enemies that try to knock you off the narrow ledges into the abyss below.

The Boss

Crash Man is a hyperactive robot who loves to jump. He is designed to react to your inputs; if you fire a shot, he will immediately jump and fire a Crash Bomber at your location. These bombs stick to walls and floors, ticking for a moment before creating a large, dangerous explosion.

Weakness and Strategy

His main weakness is the Air Shooter (from Air Man). The upward-twisting tornadoes are perfect for hitting him while he is jumping around the room. If you don't have that, the Neo Buster works, but you must be careful not to fill the room with explosions by firing too rapidly.

Pro Tip

Since Crash Man only fires when you do, you can actually control the rhythm of the fight. Walk toward a wall, fire a single shot to bait him into jumping, and then quickly move away. The bomb will hit the wall behind you and explode harmlessly while you prepare your next attack.
