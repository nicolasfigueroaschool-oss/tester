HEAT MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

The Stage

Heat Man’s stage is a literal furnace filled with pools of molten lava and disappearing blocks. The most infamous section involves a long stretch of "Yoku Blocks" over a massive lava pit. You must memorize the blinking pattern to cross, or use Item-2 (the jet board) to fly over the entire hazard.

The Boss

Heat Man behaves much like a lighter; he tosses three pillars of fire at your location. After being hit, he transforms into a flaming projectile and rocket-charges across the room. He is invulnerable while charging, so you have to jump over him and wait for him to reappear.

Weakness and Strategy

He is severely weak to the Bubble Lead (from Bubble Man). Since he stays on the ground most of the time, the bubbles roll right into him for heavy damage. Interestingly, hitting him with Atomic Fire or the Crash Bomber will actually heal him, so avoid those!

Pro Tip

When Heat Man charges at you, don't just jump straight up. Move toward him and time your jump so you land behind where he stops. This gives you a head start on dodging his next round of fire pillars and keeps you from being cornered against the wall.
