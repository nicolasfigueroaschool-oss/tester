BUBBLE MAN

The Stage

Bubble Man’s stage takes you deep underwater, where the physics change to allow for massive, floaty jumps. You must carefully navigate narrow corridors lined with instant-kill spikes and face off against giant mechanical fish that release smaller drones

The Boss

Bubble Man is the first aquatic combat robot. He attacks by encasing himself in a protective bubble and firing a rapid-fire harpoon gun. Because he stays submerged, the high-jump physics of the water make it very easy to accidentally hit the spiked ceiling while trying to dodge him.

Weakness and Strategy

Bubble Man is extremely weak to the Metal Blade. Since he often floats at awkward angles, the ability to aim the blades in eight directions makes this fight trivial. If you don't have those, the Crash Bomber is also effective at piercing his defenses.

Pro Tip

Keep your jumps short! The spiked ceiling is the biggest threat in this room. Wait for him to land before you attempt to jump over him, or you'll float straight into the spikes for an instant Game Over.
