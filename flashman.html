FLASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

The Stage

Flash Man’s stage is a slick, neon-lit cavern made of slippery ice blocks. The floor physics make it hard to stop moving once you start, and the path is filled with "Sniper Armor" mechs that take a lot of hits. There are multiple branching paths; the lower paths are harder but often contain extra lives and energy tanks.

The Boss

Flash Man is the master of time. He runs back and forth across an uneven, bumpy floor to corner you. His main ability is the Time Stopper, which freezes you in place while he fires a barrage of rapid-fire shots from his arm cannon. Because the floor is jagged, it can be tricky to jump over him as he charges.

Weakness and Strategy

He is extremely weak to the Crash Bomber (from Crash Man), which can hit him for massive damage or catch him in the blast radius if he’s near a wall. The Metal Blade is also very effective if you want to stay mobile. Since he freezes time, your best bet is to stay aggressive and deal as much damage as possible before he can trigger his ability.

Pro Tip

Flash Man’s room has a very uneven floor. Try to stand on the higher "bumps" in the terrain. This makes it much easier to jump over him when he runs toward you, as you won't need as much vertical height to clear his head.
